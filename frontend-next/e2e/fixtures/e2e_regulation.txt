E2E Synthetic Circular on Customer Verification Standards

Regulated entities shall complete customer identification at onboarding using an Officially Valid Document before opening any account.
Records of customer identification data and transactions shall be preserved for at least five years after the business relationship ends.
Every regulated entity shall appoint a Principal Officer responsible for reporting suspicious transactions to the Financial Intelligence Unit.
Regulated entities shall identify and verify the beneficial owner before establishing a relationship with a legal entity customer.
Customers shall be assigned a risk category and subjected to periodic KYC updation appropriate to that category.
